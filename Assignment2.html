Punch Some Wood

The first thing you need to do is gather some wood by holding left click. Wood is an essential material in the world of Minecraft. It is used for crafting many items throughout your minecraft journey. Any wood would do. Either oak, birch, spruce, jungle, dark oak, is all fine.

Craft Some Planks with the Wood

Now open your inventory using the E key. Place the wood block in the 2x2 grid located on the top right as shown above. Planks are essential for building and crafting weapons and tools.

Make a Crafting Table

Place the four planks back into the 2x2 grid as shown abvove. This creates a crafting table. Crafting tables creates a 3x3 grid allowing for more crafting recipes.

Craft some Sticks

Exit your inventory by pressing ESC. Select the crafting table with numbers or scrollwheel. Right-click to place down the table. Now right click again and you should see a 3x3 crafting grid instead of 2. Place two planks on top of each other shown above to craft sticks.

Make a Wooden Pickaxe

Obtain enough wood to use the recipe above. Place three planks across the top and two sticks down the middle. And you have yourself a wooden pickaxe to mine stone.
